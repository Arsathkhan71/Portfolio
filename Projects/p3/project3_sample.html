MindVibe: Predicting Mental Fitness and Recommending Activities for a Balanced Life

The MindVibe project uses machine learning to estimate mental fitness levels based on a comprehensive dataset of mental health parameters. It provides personalized activity recommendations to improve well-being and work-life balance, helping users prioritize mental health. The project aims to raise awareness and empower individuals to adopt tailored practices for a happier, healthier life.

Key Features

  • Predictive Model: Utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to predict an individual's mental fitness score based on various mental health parameters.
  • Personalized Activity Recommendations: Offers customized suggestions such as mindfulness practices, exercise routines, or work-life balance tips based on individual results to improve overall mental well-being.
  • Comprehensive Dataset: Analyzes a rich dataset that includes a wide range of mental health indicators to ensure accurate and insightful predictions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Provides an easy-to-use platform for users to input their mental health data and instantly receive insights and recommendations.
  • Actionable Insights: The model not only predicts mental fitness but also offers clear, actionable insights to enhance daily well-being.
